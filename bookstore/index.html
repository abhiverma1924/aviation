AVIATION

In the world crowded with many fields, Aviation is the most developing field providing many opportunities.. Aviation is mainly derived from the word known as “Ares ” which is a kind of animal kingdom mainly relates to bird. The governing body of whole international civil aviation industry in the world is the specialized agency of the United Nations i.e International Civil Aviation Organization(ICAO). There are total of 192 ICAO members all over the world. Major ICAO members are EASA, FAA, JAR, DGCA .
Aviation is the field related to flying aircraft, its designing, producing and maintaining them.Aviation is a field which provides not only facilities for easy transport & also provide wide opportunities of pilot, flight engineer, cabin crew, aircraft maintenance engineer..
Aviation is the that branch of engineering that is least forgiving of mistakes. Aviation is the dynamic profession. The rate of obsolescence of equipment is high and new aircraft have to be placed in inventory periodically in order to stay abreast of the requirements of modern war.
